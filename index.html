Who Is Galahad?

Galahad is the pure knight of Arthurian legend: the Round Table knight worthy to achieve the Holy Grail.

Strength of Ten

“My strength is as the strength of ten, because my heart is pure.”

The Knight

Courage without ego. Strength under control. A man who fights better because his soul is ordered.

The Faith

The Grail quest points toward Christ, grace, sacrifice, and holiness. Galahad is not an official saint, but he is a powerful Christian image of purity and purpose.

The Quest

You are not expected to be perfect. You are on the quest: train the body, guard the soul, lead the household, and become the man your kingdom needs.
